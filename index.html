This is your go-to calming tool. Use it any time you feel activated.

⚠
Get Help Zone
Systolic above 180  |  Diastolic above 110
▼
What This Means

This is higher than anything in your recent 5 months of records. Your highest was 170, so readings above 180 would be unusual for you and worth getting checked.

Call For Help If
  • • Your top number stays above 180 after 20 minutes of rest and breathing
  • • You have a sudden severe headache that feels different from your usual pressure
  • • You feel chest pain (not your usual heart beating, but actual pain or squeezing)
  • • You have trouble speaking or sudden confusion
  • • You have sudden vision changes in your good eye
What To Do
  1. Lie down
  2. Start your alternating nostril breathing
  3. Cold face washer on face and neck
  4. Re-check after 15 to 20 minutes
  5. If still above 180, or if you have any of the symptoms listed above, call Matt or call 000

Important: A high number on its own, without those danger symptoms, is usually your nervous system surging.
